🎙️ Voice & Video Labs 0.9.0 Beta 1

Narration, audio production, visual asset management, and long-form video creation in one macOS application.

Voice & Video Labs is a macOS production suite for narration, audio processing, recording, visual asset management, and long-form video creation.

This release represents the first public beta of the application formerly developed under the NVMaker name. The software has expanded far beyond its original narrated-video workflow and now brings multiple voice, audio, visual, and production systems together inside one desktop application.

⸻

⭐ Built-In Kokoro Narration Spotlight

Unlimited local narration is included after download

Voice & Video Labs comes with Kokoro text-to-speech fully integrated, packaged, and ready to use.

There is no separate Kokoro installation process.

There is no Kokoro account requirement.

There is no Kokoro API key requirement.

There is no narration subscription.

There are no per-character charges.

There are no per-minute charges.

🎧 Generate narration locally as often as you choose

After downloading Voice & Video Labs, users can generate unlimited local Kokoro narration directly on their Mac, subject only to their own hardware, storage, and production needs.

Included Kokoro features

* Approximately 25 selectable voices
* Local text-to-speech generation
* Instant voice previews
* No cloud account required
* No API key required
* No subscription required
* No usage credits required
* No per-minute narration fees
* No per-character narration fees
* Unlimited local narration after download
* Sentence-safe segment processing
* Narration checkpointing
* Interrupted-run recovery
* Existing-output validation
* Chapter and final narration assembly

Kokoro is the built-in narration engine and can be used without connecting to Azure Speech or ElevenLabs.

Azure Speech and ElevenLabs remain available as optional cloud narration providers for users who supply their own credentials.

🎬 Core Production Workflow

Voice & Video Labs can support a complete narrated-media workflow:

1. Import a DOCX or EPUB project.
2. Build sentence-safe narration segments.
3. Select Kokoro, Azure Speech, or ElevenLabs.
4. Choose and preview a narration voice.
5. Generate narration locally or through a supported cloud provider.
6. Review narration results and resume interrupted runs.
7. Generate or manage visual prompts.
8. Assign images, clips, or audio-only states to production segments.
9. Assemble a structured video project.
10. Render the final video with synchronized narration.
11. Review and manage the resulting media files.
